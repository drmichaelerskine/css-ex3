About Istanbul

Istanbul is a vibrant city that straddles Europe and Asia, creating a unique blend of cultures and influences. With its rich history, stunning architecture, and mouthwatering cuisine, it's no wonder that Istanbul attracts millions of visitors each year.

Top Attractions

Blue Mosque

The Blue Mosque, also known as Sultan Ahmed Mosque, is an iconic symbol of Istanbul. Its stunning blue tiles and grand architecture make it a must-visit attraction.

Hagia Sophia

Hagia Sophia is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the most significant architectural marvels in the world. It has served as a church, mosque, and now a museum.

Grand Bazaar

The Grand Bazaar is a bustling marketplace where you can find everything from spices and textiles to traditional crafts. It's a shopper's paradise!

Local Cuisine

Istanbul offers a diverse and delicious culinary experience. Don't miss out on trying traditional dishes like kebabs, baklava, and Turkish tea.
